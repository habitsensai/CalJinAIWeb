How Does
CalJin Work?

It's as simple as snapping a picture. CalJin uses your phone's camera and our deep AI models to estimate the volume of your food.

Then, our AI — trained on thousands of meal images — identifies each part of your dish and figures out their proportions. Finally, our powerful multimodal model analyzes it all to deliver detailed nutritional info: calories, protein, carbs, fat, and more.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 1, 2026

At CalJin AI, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our mobile application. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our application ("CalJin AI" or the "App").

By downloading, installing, or using CalJin AI, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

To provide you with personalized calorie tracking and AI-powered nutrition insights, we collect the following types of information:

A. Personal & Health Data

During the onboarding process, we ask for:

  • Gender, Age, Weight, and Height: Used to calculate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE).
  • Fitness Goals: Your target weight and desired weekly progress rate.
  • Activity Level: Information about your daily lifestyle and workout habits.

B. Nutrition & Usage Data

  • Meal Logs: Records of the food you eat, including names, calorie counts, and macronutrients (protein, carbs, fats).
  • Photos: Images of food you capture via the device camera to enable AI-powered calorie estimation. These images are sent to AI services for processing and are not permanently stored on external servers.
  • Voice Input: Audio captured via the microphone when using speech-to-text features for hands-free meal logging. Audio is processed on-device and is not stored or transmitted to external servers.

C. Device & Technical Data

  • App Usage Data: Anonymous information on how you interact with the app, such as feature usage and session duration.
  • Notification Preferences: Settings for meal reminders and goal tracking alerts.
  • Device Identifiers: Anonymous identifiers used by RevenueCat to manage your subscription status.

2. Permissions We Request

CalJin AI requests the following device permissions to deliver its features:

Permission Purpose
Camera To capture photos of food for AI-powered nutritional analysis.
Microphone To enable voice-based meal logging via speech-to-text.
Storage To save and retrieve meal photos and exported data.
Notifications To send meal reminders and goal tracking alerts.
Internet To communicate with AI services and manage subscriptions.

You can manage or revoke these permissions at any time through your device's system settings.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Personalized Planning: To generate custom calorie and macro targets based on your unique metabolic profile.
  • AI Analysis: To process images and text descriptions of food for accurate nutritional estimation.
  • Progress Tracking: To visualize your weight loss or muscle gain journey over time.
  • Subscription Management: To validate your premium subscription status and unlock Pro features.
  • App Improvement: To analyze anonymous usage patterns and optimize the user experience.

4. Third-Party Services & Data Sharing

We integrate with the following third-party services to provide core app functionality. We share only the minimum data necessary for each service to function:

Service Data Shared Purpose
Google Gemini AI Food photos, text descriptions of meals Nutritional analysis and calorie estimation
Groq AI Text-based meal and nutrition queries High-speed AI inference for nutrition coaching
RevenueCat Anonymous device/user identifiers, purchase receipts Subscription management and payment validation
Google Play Store Payment and billing information (handled by Google) In-app purchase processing

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal or health data to any third party.
  • Use your data for advertising or marketing by third parties.
  • Share your data with any parties not listed above.

Each third-party service is governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Groq Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: The majority of your personal data (health profile, meal logs, progress history) is stored locally on your device using Hive, a secure on-device database. This data is not transmitted to or stored on external servers.
  • Cloud Processing: Food photos and text queries sent to AI services (Google Gemini, Groq) are processed in real-time for nutritional analysis. These are not permanently stored on external servers after processing.
  • Subscription Data: RevenueCat stores anonymous subscription status information required to validate your premium access.
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your data both in transit (via HTTPS/TLS encryption) and at rest on your device.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

  • On-Device Data: All health profiles, meal logs, and progress data are stored locally on your device and persist until you choose to delete them.
  • Deleting Your Data: You can delete individual meal logs within the app, or clear all app data by uninstalling the app or using the "Clear Data" option in your device's App Settings.
  • Third-Party Data: Data processed by AI services is handled in real-time and is not retained. Subscription records managed by RevenueCat are subject to their own data retention policy.

If you wish to request complete deletion of any data associated with your account, please contact us at support@caljinai.com.

7. Children's Privacy

CalJin AI is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us at support@caljinai.com.

8. Your Rights & Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access & Correction: Review and update your health profile at any time within the app's settings.
  • Data Deletion: Delete your meal logs within the app, or remove all app data via your device settings or by uninstalling the app.
  • Notification Control: Enable or disable meal reminders and alerts at any time through the app or device settings.
  • Permission Management: Revoke camera, microphone, or notification permissions via your device's system settings.
  • Opt-Out of AI Processing: Stop using the camera scan or voice input features if you do not wish to send data to AI services.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this document. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Continued use of the App after any modifications constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
